General PostgreSQL efforts

PostgreSQL is an affiliated project of Software in the Public Interest. Funds donated to PostgreSQL are used to sponsor general PostgreSQL efforts. These funds are managed by the Fund raising group.

Donate to PostgreSQL

PostgreSQL Europe (PgEU)

PostgreSQL Europe is a European region non profit focusing on the support of other PostgreSQL groups in Europe. You may also use this link to donate to specific European groups such as those in Germany or the United Kingdom.

Donate to PgEU

PostgreSQL United States (PgUS)

PostgreSQL United States is a non profit for PostgreSQL in the United States. It focuses on Education, User Groups, and Advocacy.

Donate to PgUS

Tax deductibility

Please be aware that PostgreSQL contributions may or may not be tax exempt. For more information please refer to the party you are donating to and your tax professional.

Questions
